# TRADEONIX Trading Learning Library

This learning library is designed to explain trading from beginner level to advanced level in a safe, practical, and structured way.

Important: This content is for education only. It is not investment advice, financial advice, buy/sell recommendation, or guaranteed profit guidance. Markets are risky. A learner should trade only after understanding risk, using proper position sizing, and accepting that losses are part of trading.

## What Students Should Learn First

Before learning strategies, every student must understand:

- Trading is a probability game, not a certainty game.
- No strategy wins every trade.
- Risk management is more important than entry signals.
- One loss should never damage the full account.
- Beginners should practice on paper before trading real money.
- A trader must record every trade in a journal.

## Main Rule

Protect capital first. Profit comes only after discipline, patience, and process.

- "Guaranteed profit"
- "Sure shot trade"
- "Daily fixed income"

## Reference Sources For Risk Awareness

- Investor.gov explains investor education, due diligence, diversification, and fraud awareness.
